Refund & Cancellation Policy


Cancellations

  • If you order has not yet been confirmed, we will cancel it at no charge.
  • Once your order has been confirmed by the chef, we will only accept cancellation requests before the chef has started preparing your item (typically within 10 minutes). Please call us if you need to cancel your order after confirmation.

Refunds

  • Due to the nature of fresh food delivery, we are unable to provide returns / refunds on delivered items.
  • If we have already received a payment from you and for any reason if we are unable to fulfill your order, we will provide a full refund within 7 business days using the original method of payment.
